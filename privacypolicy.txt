Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24/10/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the application Cardboard Panorama Viewer (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.
No personal information, identifiers, analytics data, or device information are gathered or shared with third parties.

2. Local Processing

All image and audio processing takes place entirely on your device.
The App only saves temporary files in its private storage area, which are not accessible to other applications and are automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Internet and Network Access

The App does not use the Internet, cloud services, or any external servers.
All functions are performed offline.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising services.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Security

Because no personal data is transmitted or stored externally, the App does not expose any security risk to your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future (for example, if new features require network access), we will update this page and the effective date accordingly.
